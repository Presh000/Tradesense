About Us

TradSense® is a route to market automation and trade intelligence services provider.

    We offer a comprehensive platform and services that deliver valuable business insights and practical intelligence derived from untapped consumer clusters and trade channels. Our primary objective is to equip your organization with detailed and current market information, facilitating sales and distribution activities across diverse categories and company scales. Powered by our robust Platform as a Service (PaaS) engine, TradeSense®, we ensure that your team gains a deep understanding of the market to enhance sales performance.
